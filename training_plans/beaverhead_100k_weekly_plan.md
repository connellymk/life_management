## STRENGTH TRAINING PROTOCOL

**Weeks 1-8 (Base):** 3x per week, 15-20 min bodyweight/minimal equipment
- Focus: Movement patterns, stability, injury prevention

**Weeks 9-17 (Build):** 2x per week, 20 min bodyweight OR 1x per week 45-60 min gym-based
- Focus: Maintain strength, power development, core stability

**Weeks 18-20 (Peak):** 1-2x per week, 15-20 min maintenance
- Focus: Maintain strength without adding fatigue

**Weeks 21-24 (Taper):** 1x per week, 15 min light maintenance
- Focus: Maintain neuromuscular readiness

---

## KEY TRAINING PRINCIPLES (Koop/Browning Methods)

1. **Time-on-feet over pace:** Most runs prescribed by time, not pace
2. **Vertical gain emphasis:** Progressive vertical loading throughout plan
3. **Back-to-back long runs:** Simulate race-day fatigue
4. **Specificity:** Train on similar terrain and altitude when possible
5. **Recovery weeks:** Every 3-4 weeks, reduce volume by 30-40%
6. **Strength integration:** Consistent injury prevention work
7. **Flexible scheduling:** Weather and life happen, adapt as needed
8. **Nutrition practice:** Every long run is nutrition rehearsal
